Who I Am

Photographer | Visionary | Artist

Photography has been a big part of my life for as long as I can remember. Growing up in beautiful Northeast Ohio, I was fortunate to experience all four seasons. I began snapping pictures of the changes in nature every season. From that moment forward I knew that photography wasn't just a hobby. I knew it would be a part of me for the rest of my life.

I earned my BFA from Kent State University. Afterwards I wanted to explore the entrepreneurial aspects of photography. I wanted to be a part of the creative process entirely from start to finish. Opening my own photography studio has afforded me the opportunity to not only do what I love, which is photography, but I get to touch and inspire people through art. Life is a finite experience, but art tells a story that lasts forever.
